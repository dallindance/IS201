About Me

This is my story.

Spokane based DJ/Producer Dallin Dance interlaces euphoric melodies with emotive house music. He creates eccentrically arranged EDM tracks which range from catchy, pop-house to more chill, ambient downtempo songs. He experienced a rise to recognition after producing tracks for multiple top-charting hip/ hop artists. Raised in small-town Coeur d’Alene, Dallin Dance made his first foray in music at a very young age, taking up piano at age 6 and guitar at age 9. By 7th grade, Dallin was performing with the high-school jazz band in renowned festivals around the Northwest. Years of classical piano and guitar training provided a foundation for Dallin to pursue his true passion - writing/ producing tracks. After his aunt introduced him to music software, Dallin was immediately hooked. He began producing hip/ hop and downtempo beats, eventually landing placements with a few top-charting hip/hop artists. Dallin explored making his own music but didn’t feel confident releasing it until he began DJ-ing. Later performing with artists such as Cheat Codes and Autograf during their tour, he enjoyed performing his own tracks and remixes. Finally at age 18, Dallin began releasing his first few singles.
